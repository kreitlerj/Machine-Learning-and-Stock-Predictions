Predict a Stock's Closing Price Using a Neural Network!

Project Description:

An LSTM Model is a recurrent neural network (RNN) and over time, RNNS have proved themselves as one of the most powerful models for processing sequential data. In our case this is time series data.

An LSTM Model introduces the concept of a "memory cell". This is a unit of computation that replaces traditional artificial neurons in the hidden layer of the network.

Our goal is to predict as accurately as possible the closing stock price for a given stock using three models fit on 10, 30 and 60 day intervals.
